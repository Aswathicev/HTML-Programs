college of engineering vadakara



College of Engineering Vadakara, Kerala was formerly known as the Co-operative Institute of Technology. CEV Vadakara offers UG and PG engineering courses to students through B.Tech and MCA degrees. There are a total of 6 specializations in B.tech namely, Civil, Computer Science, Electrical and Electronics, Electronics and Communication, Electronics & Instrumentation, and Information Technology. Admission to the college is entrance and quota-based. To apply for admission, students must fill the application form online on the official website and submit the completed form at the campus. Graduates from the college are placed in top-notch companies such as Infosys, Infotech, Amazon, Wipro, TCS, etc. with a handsome amount of money as their salary package
